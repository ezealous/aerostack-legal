Privacy Policy

AeroStack ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by Outdoor Hub LLC, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AeroStack mobile application.

Please read this policy carefully. By using AeroStack, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly: Card names and issuers, point balances you enter manually, travel preferences, home airport code, trip purpose selections, and account credentials.
  • Optional bank connection data (via Plaid): If you choose to connect a financial account, we receive read-only access to transaction data through Plaid Inc. We never receive or store your bank login credentials.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, app version, and crash reports to improve app stability.
  • Usage data: Features you interact with, search queries made within the app (not transmitted externally), and session duration.

✅ We do not store your raw bank passwords, Social Security numbers, or full credit card numbers. Point balances and preferences are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate your True Net Cost for flights, hotels, and cruise redemptions
  • Track benefit usage and send expiry alerts before credits are lost
  • Send price drop notifications for tracked trips
  • Personalize Popular Routes and Trip Purpose recommendations based on your home airport
  • Auto-detect and activate card offers (Amex Offers, Chase Offers) when enabled
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs using anonymized crash data

3. Plaid Integration READ-ONLY

AeroStack offers optional bank account connectivity powered by Plaid Inc. This connection is strictly limited to:

  • Product scope: transactions only
  • Purpose: Automatically detecting card benefit usage so you never manually track credits
  • What we never request: Auth (account numbers), Identity, Assets, Investments, or Liabilities products

⚠️ We never initiate transfers, payments, or modifications to your financial accounts. Our Plaid integration is permanently read-only.

You can revoke Plaid access at any time by going to Settings → Data → Disconnect Bank. Revoking access immediately terminates our ability to read your transactions. You can also revoke access directly at my.plaid.com.

By connecting your bank account, you also agree to Plaid's Privacy Policy at plaid.com/legal.

4. Data Security

  • All data transmitted between AeroStack and our servers uses TLS 1.2 or higher encryption
  • Sensitive values (Plaid access tokens, authentication tokens) are stored using expo-secure-store, which leverages iOS Keychain and Android Keystore
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID) gates access to financial screens
  • We do not sell your personal information to any third parties
  • We conduct regular dependency vulnerability scans via GitHub Dependabot

5. Third-Party Services

AeroStack integrates with the following services:

  • Plaid Inc. — Optional bank connectivity (read-only transactions)
  • RapidAPI / Duffel — Live flight, hotel, and cruise pricing data (no PII transmitted)
  • Expo / React Native — Push notification delivery and app infrastructure

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We are not responsible for their data practices.

6. Data Retention

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed. If you connected Plaid, your access token is revoked upon account deletion. We do not maintain server-side user profiles in the current version of AeroStack.

7. Children's Privacy

AeroStack is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data at any time via Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account
  • Opt out of push notifications via iOS Settings
  • Revoke Plaid bank connection at any time
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing us

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@aerostack.app.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via push notification or in-app alert. Continued use of AeroStack after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about your privacy?

We take privacy seriously and respond within 48 hours.
